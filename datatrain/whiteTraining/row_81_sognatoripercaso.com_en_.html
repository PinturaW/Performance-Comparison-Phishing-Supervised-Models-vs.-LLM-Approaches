Wedding Music – How Many to Choose?


Firstly, we would like to say that the question above does not have an exact answer, as not all ceremonies will be exactly the same.

With the same number of inputs, the same number of moments…

A number of songs What will play at your wedding depends a lot on what you want.

Mainly, from your wedding proposal, providing the stages of your ceremony. From simple weddings to luxury weddings.

The number of songs to be played will depend on the number of stages your ceremony will go through.

For example, some ceremonies have some moments and rituals that not all ceremonies perform. And you are the one who will define this. Along with your fiancé, of course.

And then, according to the script of your ceremony and your musical taste, it is up to you to take the information to the Wedding Choir that you hired.

And talk about what are the ceremonial passages, and in which of them there will be music and in which of them there will be no need for an orchestra.

However, it is clear that, once the Wedding Choir is hired, it is best to make the most of it and explore it at each stage of your wedding.

The important thing is to have a plan, however simple it may be, but this decision must be made by the bride and groom.

Properly guided and assisted by someone from the Choir who has the expertise.

It may be that you want the participation of the Choir at all times of the wedding, or that at certain moments only the participation of the Orchestra in an instrumental way.

Therefore, we reinforce that the most important thing is that you are 100% confident and satisfied with these choices when making the decision, and finally finding out how many songs will be played in total in your Wedding.

Committing to a more caring world

The mission of the men and women of Auchan Retail is to ensure that everyone around us can live better. Through our Vision 2032 "Eat well, live better while preserving the planet", we want to build a better, more humane world. Anchored in the daily life of our territories, we see that the world is changing and that needs are evolving. How much our customers, partners, suppliers and employees expect new practices. They are counting on Auchan Retail, on us, to support them.

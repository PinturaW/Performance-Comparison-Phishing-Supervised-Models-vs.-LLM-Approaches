A practical guide for turning your data into a profitable asset

Download now

Build data products with precision and control

Curate and tailor bespoke data products for any business need. Tap into powerful insights, fuel data monetization strategies, and deploy cutting-edge data products with ease.

  • Secure data connections: Integrate seamlessly across distributed cloud and on-premises data warehouses and analytics tools.
  • Privacy-first controls: Mask, redact, and transform data with precision to meet specific business requirements.
  • Granular permission controls: Share data products securely with internal and external stakeholders.

Monetize your data with ease

List your data products on popular marketplaces, gain valuable insights into customer demand, and accelerate deals with seamless integrations to existing sales and marketing tools.

  • Vendia native app for Snowflake: Leverage our native Snowflake application to gain deeper marketplace insights and understand your customers.
  • Data marketplace onboarding support: Our team provides expert guidance and support to get your data products listed and generating revenue quickly.

Distribute data with confidence, anywhere

Effortlessly deliver data products to any line of business, customer, or partner, anywhere in the world. Vendia ensures secure, compliant, and auditable data distribution across regions and warehouses, no matter the scale.

  • Data delivery for leading data warehouses: Seamlessly move large datasets to Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, Databricks, and more, in formats like Iceberg and Delta.
  • Automated compliance: Apply appropriate data privacy and residency rules automatically.
  • End-to-end visibility: Gain complete insight into data lineage and audit trails for every data product.

With Vendia, our sales team now has a complete data picture, synced across our stack. This visibility empowers them to make decisive moves and accelerate deal conversions.

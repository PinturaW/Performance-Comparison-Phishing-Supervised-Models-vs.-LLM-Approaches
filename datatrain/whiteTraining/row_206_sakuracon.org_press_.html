Press Application Form

Sakura-Con welcomes and appreciates all media coverage. Please complete the press application below to request press access to our event. You may be contacted with questions in the process of determining approval, so please be sure to add press@sakuracon.org to your email address book or email white list in order to receive emails from the Sakura-Con Press staff.

If you are a member of the press, or press outlet looking to cover Sakura-Con 2025 with access to guest interviews, you may apply here.

If you are interested in applying for a Media Badge, which will grant you access to Sakura-Con at a discounted rate, you may apply here. Media Badges do not include access to guest interviews.

Sakura-Con does not guarantee approval on any interview requests. Specifically approved press teams will receive further instructions on how to request access to guests for interviews, subject to approval and availability of our guests. Press members may be asked for further information for an interview if they are approved for one.

All applications must be received via our form by February 28, 2025. Sakura-Con does not guarantee all applicants will be granted press access but will respond to all applications that are received before February 28, 2025. Any requests received after that date will not be approved.

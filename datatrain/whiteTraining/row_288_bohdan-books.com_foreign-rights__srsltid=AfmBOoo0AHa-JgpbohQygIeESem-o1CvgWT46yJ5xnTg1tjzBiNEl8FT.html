BOHDAN PUBLISHING HOUSE (FOUNDED 1997)

Founded in 1997 in Ternopil, Bohdan Publishing House is one of Ukraine’s leading publishing houses. The company has released over 5,000 book titles, focusing on three key areas:

  • Fiction – global bestsellers (Sci-fi, fantasy, horror, literary fiction, mystery/crime, thriller, detective, true crime, historical fiction, romance, YA, new adult, graphic novels).
  • Children’s Books – works by Ukrainian and international authors (picture books, early readers, chapter books, middle grade, fables, nonfiction, activity books).
  • Educational Literature – textbooks, reference books, dictionaries, and methodological guides for all levels.

Key Achievements:

  • Collaboration with top Ukrainian and international authors and translators.
  • Own bookstore network "Dim Knyhy" and well-established distribution across Ukraine and abroad.
  • Participation in international book fairs and partnerships with foreign publishers.
  • Active social engagement: literary events, author meetings, charity projects.

With a commitment to quality and innovation, Bohdan Publishing House continues to shape the future of Ukrainian education and culture.

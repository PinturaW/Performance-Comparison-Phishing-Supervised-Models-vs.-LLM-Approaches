Your questions, answered
Is HousingAnywhere a rental agency?
No, we're not a real estate agency. HousingAnywhere.com is an online platform connecting people looking for their next home with landlords looking for tenants.
My rental application was accepted. What's next?
You'll pay the first month's rent and, depending on the region, the Tenant Protection fee. Once we've received your payment, your booking is confirmed. You'll then get the landlord's contact details. You can pay the rest of the rental costs directly to the landlord.
What if I want to cancel my booking?
You can cancel your booking at any time before you move in. Depending on when you cancel and the cancellation policy that applies to your booking, you can receive a full or partial refund. The Tenant Protection fee is non-refundable.
Can I visit the place before I book?
No, and you won't need to. Listings have detailed descriptions, photos, videos, and floor plans to give you a clear idea of what you'll get. Through our platform, you can message the landlord, clear doubts, and even exchange documents. And once you book, your money is safe with us until you move in.

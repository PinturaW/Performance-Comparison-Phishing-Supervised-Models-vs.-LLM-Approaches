    Animal welfare is everyone's welfare

    For two centuries, weve improved millions of animals lives. But as the world changes, new challenges arise. We cant face them alone. Lets work together to build a better future for every animal.

    Donate now
    Who we are

    Appeal

    Help stop cruelty this summer

    Beyond the garden fence and behind closed doors, thousands of animals suffer in silence. This is the summer you don't see. Your donation can help us reach them and protect them from cruelty.

    Donate to stop cruelty

    Help transform animals' lives

    Your time, action and love could save animals lives and build a better future for every kind. Find out how you can help us raise funds, give animals a voice and rescue and care for animals who give us so much. Lets show them we care. 

    Raise money

    Enter our events, create your own challenge or help a young person become an Animal Hero.

    Fundraise

    Give your time

    Volunteer from home or in your area its a great way to learn new skills and meet animal lovers.

    Volunteer

    Inspire young people

    Inspire young minds with our engaging resources, to prevent cruelty and promote compassion.

    Educate

    Take action

    Your voice matters. Get clued up on animal welfare and help us change laws, industries and minds.

    Campaign

    Together for animals

    The future of food and farming

    Low welfare farming is the single biggest animal welfare issue on the planet. As part of our refreshed strategy to 2030, we outline the need to reduce the dependency on low welfare farming.
    Read our approach to food and farming

    Kindness Index results

    Discover how the public really thinks and acts when it comes to our pets.

    Pet insurance

    Get peace of mind with our pet insurance cover.

    Dogs die in hot cars, dont risk it!

    Learn the signs of heatstroke and what to do in an emergency.

    Keep pets safe this summer

    Make sure you're prepared to keep your animals safe in the heat.

    Help animals like Babs

    This spring, help animals like Babs. She was left outside without food or water after giving birth. With our care, she overcame challenges and found a new home.

    Demanding change for CO2 on pigs

    We're pushing hard for the UK government to provide a more humane alternative to CO2 gas slaughter for pigs. Discover the steps we've already taken and how we're fighting for change.

    Write Your Will for Free

    Protect your loved ones and support your favourite causes. Setting up an appointment is easy - just fill out our simple form today.

    Where your money goes

    Each year, we answer more than a million calls about animals and we rescue thousands of animals. But this is only possible with your help. We rely on donations to fund our vital services.

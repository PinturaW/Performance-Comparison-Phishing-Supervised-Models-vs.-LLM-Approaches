Keeping your cat happy and healthy

The more we understand cats, the better we can care for them.

Find out more

At International Cat Care we are passionate about improving the welfare of cats throughout the world.

Just like you, we love cats and want to make the world better for them. 
 
Our team of experts, from feline medicine specialists to researchers in cat welfare, wellbeing and behaviour, work with people who share our vision of a cat friendly world and can have a positive impact on the lives of cats – veterinary professionals, shelters and homing organisations, animal welfare charities, central governments, breeders and cat owners or caregivers.
 
We share expert knowledge and create programmes that bring positive change to the lives of cats everywhere. Better knowledge leads to better care for cats.

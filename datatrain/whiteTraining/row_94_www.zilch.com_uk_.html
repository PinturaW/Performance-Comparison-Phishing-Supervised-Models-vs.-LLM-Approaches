            How do Zilch Rewards Work?

            When you make a Pay now purchase using Zilch, youll get up to 5% back in Rewards. You can save them up or spend them straight away online or in-store. Rewards are credited to your account instantly, so you can keep a tab on how much youre earning youll see yours racking up in the top right corner of the app.

          • +

            How do I sign up?

            You can sign up to Zilch in just a couple of taps. First, download the Zilch app via the App Store or Google Play Store, then tap Create account and follow the simple instructions. Well run a soft credit check and verify your ID. Once youre approved, youll be able to start spending same day.

          • +

            How do I get in touch?

            Our Customer Support team is here to help seven days a week. You can speak to them via Live Chat in the app (9am6pm) or give us a call on 020 3405 9775 (9am5pm).

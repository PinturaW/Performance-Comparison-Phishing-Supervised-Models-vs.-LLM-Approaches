FAQs

Applying for Zebit

  • Registering Using Internet Explorer

    Just go to www.zebit.com/register to start a new application.

    You can attempt to register up to three (3) times, per day, per email. Since no FICO score is needed, there is no risk to re-apply. Any variation on the match-up of information and the system will not be able to perform the verification.

  • Why do you need my Social Security Number?

    We use your SSN to verify your identity and perform our own assessment of your creditworthiness. Your SSN is protected and securely encrypted.

  • I applied and I was declined. Can I apply again?

    While you can apply again, we encourage you to review the Adverse Action Notice you received in order to understand why you were denied.

Credit

  • Do I have to have good credit to be approved?

    We do not require a FICO score to qualify for credit to shop at Zebit. Instead, we look at various factors from the specialty credit reporting agencies and our own criteria to determine your eligibility. 

    Not all applicants will be approved for credit.

  • Do you report credit?

    Zebit does not report payment history to the major credit bureaus of Experian, TransUnion or Equifax. For customers who applied after July 2021, we report to Clarity Services, a specialty consumer reporting agency.

  • Do you check credit?

    Zebit does not pull a FICO score nor make an inquiry that impacts your FICO score.  We do pull data from certain specialty credit reporting agencies to validate information such as your identity, employment and/or income to determine how much credit we can offer you to shop on Zebit.  This may appear as a soft pull on your credit report but does not affect your FICO credit score.  Not all applicants will be approved for credit. 

  • What agency do you perform a credit check with?

    The information we use when performing a credit check is kept and maintained by SageStream or Clarity Services. If you received an Adverse Action Notice, it states the company we used.  You have a right under the Fair Credit Reporting Act to know the information contained in your file. Should you wish to obtain a copy of this information you may contact the company noted at:

Zebit Market

  • What is Zebit Market?

    The Zebit Market is an eCommerce store where you can use your Spending Limit to buy a wide range of quality, name brand products.

  • What types of goods are sold in the Zebit Market?

    The Zebit Market carries a variety of products across many categories including, but not limited to: electronics, computers, furniture, appliances, health equipment, home goods, kitchenware, and accessories. We are constantly updating the products within the Zebit Market.

  • Are the goods name brand, new, used and/or refurbished?

    The products are new and in their original packaging. If we do offer open-box or refurbished items, these items will be explicitly marked as such in the Zebit Market.

  • Why are prices listed as both Pay-In-Full and Per Payment?

    Items we sell only have one price. You can choose whether to purchase items outright or pay in installments. We reflect figures for either option to provide customers with information to make their decisions.

    Total payments will never exceed the purchase price inclusive of all tax and shipping.

  • How do I select a payment option at checkout?

    At checkout you will see multiple payment options in your shopping cart.

    You may select the option that best fits your financing needs.

Bohdan Publishing House was established in 1997 in Ternopil, Ukraine. Major activities: publication of Ukrainian and translated fiction literature for children and teenagers, art books, toy books, popular scientific literature, educational and teaching manuals for primary, middle and high schools, dictionaries, reference books and encyclopedias; creation e-books.

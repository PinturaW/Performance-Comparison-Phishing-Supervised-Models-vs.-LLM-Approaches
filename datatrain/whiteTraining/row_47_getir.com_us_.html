Getir is an ultra-fast grocery delivery service on your phone.

Our mission is all about providing convenience without compromise: delivering high-quality, fresh, and affordable groceries sustainably within 15-20 minutes.

You can get all your everyday grocery essentials in one tap.

You'll find more than 2,000 products on our app, including thousands of fresh items, snacks, beverages, alcohol, and more. Our stock is replenished every morning, ensuring a continually fresh and diverse selection. Keep an eye out for great deals on our app.

We mean when we say no substitutions ever.

What sets us apart is that we own our stores, and unlike other apps you may be familiar with, we take immense pride in owning the entire service, from inventory management to the delivery process. In addition to our exceptional range and speed, we pride ourselves on being 100% reliable. Our technology enables us to maintain real-time inventory, ensuring that we serve you without any substitutions. Our packers handle your items with care, and our riders deliver with precision.

We’ll be at your door in minutes.

Our stores are strategically located within a few miles from your delivery address. This proximity is the key to our riders arriving at your doorstep within just 20 minutes. We operate with an electric fleet and take pride in upholding the highest standards of worker welfare. The majority of our delivery team is our employees who receive benefits.

Additional information

In order to subscribe to Litres, it is required to dial the relevant USSD code::

  • To subscribe to the "Standard" daily plan, dial *288#YES. The service fee is 0.15 AZN.
  • To subscribe to the "Standard" weekly plan, dial *299#YES. The service fee is 1 AZN.
  • To subscribe to the "Standard" monthly plan, dial *301#YES. The service fee is 4 AZN.
  • To subscribe to the "Audio" daily plan, dial *444#YES. The service fee is 0.23 AZN.
  • To subscribe to the "Audio" weekly plan, dial *454#YES. The service fee is 1.50 AZN.
  • To subscribe to the "Audio" monthly plan, dial *464#YES. The service fee is 6 AZN.

To activate the application:

  1. Dial the USSD code;
  2. Follow the link sent by SMS;
  3. Download the app; 
  4. After downloading the application, select the "Log in/Register" button; 
  5. You need to register by indicating your Azercell mobile number and the code sent to your smartphone via SMS.

Litres Standard package includes 140 000 e-books and the Litres Audio pack includes 50 000 audiobooks.

On "Litres" you can find more than 1500 books on various topics and genres in the Azerbaijani language.

  • To cancel the subscription to the "Standard" daily plan, send an SMS with the text Stop gun to the number 4444;
  • To cancel the subscription to the weekly "Standard" plan, send SMS with the text Stop hefte to the number 4444;
  • To cancel the subscription to the monthly "Standard" plan, send an SMS with the text Stop ay to the number 4444;
  • To cancel the subscription to the daily "Audio" plan, send SMS with the text Stop audio gun to the number 4444;
  • To cancel the subscription to the weekly "Audio" plan, send SMS with the text Stop audio hefte to the number 4444;
  • To cancel the subscription to the monthly "Audio" plan, send SMS with the text Stop audio ay to the number 4444.

You can’t deactivate the service by deleting the app and the subscription fee will automatically be charged every month.

Litres is an internet based mobile application. The application can be used if the internet package is active on the number or by connecting to a WiFi network.

You can continue to use the mobile application even after the internet pack has expired. In this case, the fee for each MB is calculated according to the tariff plan.

    Ripol-Classic


    RIPOL Classic - 33 years with you!

    Books are our life! We read at work, on trips and at home. In each of our vacations, we find unusual authors. We follow literary trends on all continents and look for exciting and unusual stories. Bright novelties, conceptual series and books that give new knowledge and new emotions - all this is in our publishing portfolio.  

    Librarium is a series featuring rare and popular classics of world literature and philosophical thought.

    Crime fiction and Beautiful Things are series where we publish action novels, exciting thrillers and detective stories, exciting fantasy and horror films, exciting romance novels and rom-coms, Scandinavian noirs.

    The Horror Story series is a classic of world horror literature, on which, no doubt, Stephen King himself grew up.

    The New Horror series are modern, chilling horror films for suspense lovers, written with respect for the authors of bygone eras.

    The Roots and Salt series is a metaphorical tale about growing up and your purpose in life.

    A series of "Metamorphoses" - publications similar to works of art.

    The Playlist series is the most interesting books about music.

    We publish world-famous authors: Jeffrey Eugenides, Elizabeth Gilbert, Eckhart Tolle, Robert Green, Herbjerg Wassmo, Jean Baudrillard, Slavoj Zizek, Alain Badiou, Niklas Nutt-au-Dag, Bridget Collins, Alberto-Vazquez Figueroa, Federico Moccia, Janelle Brown, Stina Jackson, Liv Constantine, Luz Gabas, Douglas Kennedy, Jan Fabre, Benjamin Lacombe, Ellis Peters, Gerd Nygårdshaug.

    We have opened up new names for our readers: Sylvia Moreno-Garcia, Harriet Theis, Deirdre Sullivan, Elena Forbes, Anna Carolina, Chan Ho-Key, Lex Crouch, Victoria Walters, Anna Benning, Megan Hunter, Nina Vyaha.

    A special place is occupied by the texts of Russian authors: Mikhail Epstein, Alexei Ivanov, Oleg Roy, Alexei Sitnikov, Mikhail Khazin, Vladimir Ryzhkov, Alexander Zvyagintsev, Vladimir Alenikov, Irada Berg, Sergey Markov, Alexander Smulyansky, Natalya Laydinen.

    In the summer of 2021, a separate editorial office "RIPOL Childhood" was formed at the publishing house, which loves books and creates magic. Miracles, magic, adventures, mysteries and brave young heroes - with us you can go on an exciting journey through many incredible worlds! Flying to the moon, the catacombs of Ancient Egypt, the mysteries of the impenetrable jungle, the secrets of the deep sea ... and much more amazing things in books that will definitely not let you get bored.

    We create books that contain KNOWLEDGE, UNDERSTANDING, MOOD - three great powers that, according to Merab Mamardashvili, are necessary for every human being to feel alive.

Moo Moo The Wine Bar + Grill:

Experience Steak, like never before!

For two decades, Moo Moo the Wine Bar + Grill at Broadbeach has been a cornerstone of family dining on the Gold Coast, renowned for its unparalleled beef offerings. Its reputation for delivering flawlessly crafted steaks remains unmatched.

At Moo Moo, our beef dishes take centre stage, showcasing a diverse selection of premium cuts from both local and international sources. Some cuts are exclusive to Moo Moo, making it the ultimate destination for those who value exceptional quality and flavour in family dining Gold Coast.

Consistent Excellence

Through Relationships

At Moo Moo, our enduring partnerships with top producers ensure a consistent supply of exclusive cuts and high marble scores, setting us apart from restaurants that frequently switch suppliers. These strong relationships allow Moo Moo to maintain unmatched consistency and exclusivity, making us a premier choice for family dining Gold Coast experiences you won’t find anywhere else.

A World-Class Menu

With 20 beef cuts available at any time, sourced from across Australia, Moo Moo offers one of the most comprehensive selections of beef imaginable. From pasture-fed options to ultra-marbled Wagyu imported from Japan, every cut is chosen to reflect the pinnacle of quality, solidifying our place among the top five-star restaurants Gold Coast diners seek for an exceptional steak experience.

While Moo Moo is renowned for its exceptional steaks, it also offers a diverse selection of fresh seafood, perfectly cooked poultry, and delicious game. Complementing its outstanding cuisine is an award-winning wine list featuring over 900 carefully curated wines, showcasing Australia’s finest vintages alongside iconic international labels.

A Passion for Quality

Moo Moo bridges the gap between producers and diners, bringing their passion and dedication directly to your plate. Each dish reflects the extraordinary effort and commitment that goes into sourcing the finest beef. Learn more about us and our unique approach.

At Moo Moo, the kitchen is a playground for our chefs, offering the freedom to experiment with the best ingredients and techniques. This approach is why every visit delivers an unforgettable dining experience at our restaurant and Moobar.

Why Choose MOO MOO?

  • Exceptional beef from world-class producers.
  • Exclusive partnerships with breeders and suppliers.
  • One of the most extensive steak and wine menus in Australia.
  • A steadfast commitment to quality, with cuts and marble scores allocated exclusively to Moo Moo.
  • A diverse menu that also features fresh seafood, expertly prepared poultry, and premium game.

When you dine at Moo Moo The Wine Bar + Grill, you’re not just enjoying a meal— you’re experiencing a legacy of excellence built on relationships, expertise, and a relentless pursuit of perfection.

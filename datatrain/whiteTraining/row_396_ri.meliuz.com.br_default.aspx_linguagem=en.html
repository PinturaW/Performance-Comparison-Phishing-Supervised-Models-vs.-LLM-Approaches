        CASH3: Frequently Asked Questions

        MÉLIUZ S.A. (B3:CASH3) was the first startup to go public on the Brazilian Stock Exchange (B3), marking the beginning of the CASH3 Group.
        Méliuz is a company that provides digital solutions to connect brands and stores with end consumers through our shopping vertical (marketplace) and financial services vertical. With over 13 years in the market, we currently have more than 35 million registered accounts and over 1,000 active partners.

        Méliuz, Picodi, Melhor Plano, Muambator and Promobit.

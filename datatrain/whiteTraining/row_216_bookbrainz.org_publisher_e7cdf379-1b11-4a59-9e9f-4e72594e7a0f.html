Annotation

British book publishing house.
Acquired by Houghton Mifflin in 1989.
Sold to Cassell & Co, a subsidiary of The Orion Publishing Group Limited in October 1992.
Turned into a science fiction and fantasy imprint of the Orion Publishing Group in December 1998.

RADIA-31

TYING MACHINE

The Radia-31 sausage tying machine incorporates new technological advancements and an innovative design to optimize the production process, adapting to various types of casing and calibers. It delivers high-quality finishes with maximum productivity and combines cutting-edge technology with an artisanal presentation to meet the needs of a constantly evolving market.

THE COMPANY

At Osnova, we design and manufacture advanced solutions for the food industry, specializing in the meat sector. With over 30 years of experience, we have established ourselves as a benchmark in sausage tying machines, designed for companies aiming to highlight their products with an artisanal and high-quality presentation. We adapt to market needs, offering innovations that optimize processes and enhance the value of their products.

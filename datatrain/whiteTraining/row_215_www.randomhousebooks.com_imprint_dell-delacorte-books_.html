Delacorte/Dell

Our oldest imprint, Dell was founded by George T. Delacorte in 1921. Dell has always been synonymous with the best of popular storytelling and now publishes such big brand names as Lee Child, Diana Gabaldon, and Danielle Steel. The Delacorte/Dell lists focus on general fiction, love stories, and romantic comedy and are committed to nurturing voices that will allow us to continue our century-long tradition of sharing beloved books with the world.

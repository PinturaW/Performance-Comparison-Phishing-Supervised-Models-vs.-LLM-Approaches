To send a package internationally as individual, contact a CDEK office in your country. Pick a convenient method in our "Contacts" section: phone or e-mail. CDEK employees will consult with you and pick the best option.

Countries CDEK operates in:
Kazakhstan, Belarus, Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, Azerbaijan, Tajikistan, China, Thailand, Vietnam, India, Bangladesh, Mongolia, Indonesia, Serbia, Turkey, UAE, Abkhazia, Israel.

USA, South Korea, Moldova, Poland, Italy, Spain, Germany, Bulgaria, Lithuania, Great Britain, France, Finland, Cyprus, Czech Republic, Hungary, Estonia.
For individuals, parcels and documents can be sent. CDEK does not deliver dangerous cargo or objects that are forbidden to be shipped by law. Find the full list in the "Forbidden goods" section. If you are not sure if your cargo is forbidden, contact us by phone or e-mail in the "Contacts" section.
By using CDEK Forward, our service for delivery of packages from international online stores. After registering, you will have access to addresses of CDEK warehouses in 20 countries around the world. Purchase from international stores with delivery to a local CDEK office. We will receive your orders, put them in single package to lower price of delivery, and send them to your country.

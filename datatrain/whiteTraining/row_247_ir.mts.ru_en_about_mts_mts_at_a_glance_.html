Who we are

MTS is a Russian digital ecosystem and the country’s largest mobile operator, which provides a wide range of digital-first services for individuals and business customers. Our ecosystem is developing on the basis of MTS’s leading Telecom business and dozens of fast-growing products and business verticals, the largest of which are FinTech, Retail and Media. 

Over 86 million mobile subscribers use our services in Russia and Belarus, including around 81 million subscribers in Russia. Our ecosystem customer base (customers actively and deliberately using at least two different MTS products or services from different categories) has more than 14 million customers.

We are broadening the definition of Telecom with innovative offering across big data, fintech, web services, media, Internet of Things, Adtech, e-commerce and other areas. We leverage our market-leading retail network as a platform for additional customer services and sales of devices and accessories.

MTS has been listed on the Moscow Exchange since November 2003 (MOEX: MTSS). The public free float of the Company’s shares is approximately 42.5%. More information about the shareholder structure can be found on MTS IR

MTS Brand

Our brand is one of our most critical assets, signifying trust on the part of our customers, vendors and ecosystem partners.

In 2023, we presented our first ecosystem branding, which provides an overarching framework for dozens of products and businesses beyond connectivity.

Our brand reflects the loyalty of our customers, the pride of our employees and our reputation as Russia’s leading telecoms provider and digital ecosystem.

About Astrolabe Publishing

The Astrolabe Publishing is a Ukrainian publishing house (founded in 2000 in Lviv) producing fiction as well as books on philosophy, psychology, history, archaeology, law, political science, economics and business, military literature, mainly in Ukrainian. Among the authors of the publishing house there are Aristotle, J. Bator, P. Calderón de la Barca, C. Ph. G. von Clausewitz, H. von Dach, Dante, G. Deleuze, F. Guattari, J. Konrad, L. Krushelnytska, F. Lope de Vega y Carpio, C. G. E. Mannerheim, K. F. May, F. Nietzsche, J. R. R. Tolkien, M. de Unamuno, K. Čapek, C. G. Jung, Ts. Yamamoto and others. The periodical Lviv military almanac The Citadel is also one of our important projects.

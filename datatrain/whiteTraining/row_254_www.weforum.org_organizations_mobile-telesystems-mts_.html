Mobile Telesystems (MTS)

Mobile Telesystems is a leading telecommunications group in Russia, Eastern Europe and Central Asia, offering mobile and fixed voice, broadband, pay TV as well as content and entertainment services. as of 31 December 2010, the group serviced over 108.1 million mobile subscribers in Russia, Ukraine, Uzbekistan, Turkmenistan, Armenia and Belarus, the group's fixed business had a total of 9.9 million households, 1.8 million broadband Internet and 2.6 million pay-TV subscribers and its revenues reached US$ 11.3 billion.

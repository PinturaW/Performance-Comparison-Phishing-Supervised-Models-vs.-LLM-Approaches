ALDEBARAN

It’s a white light in the darkness. A mysterious brightness radiating from a nocturnal clearing. The caress of the moon on a field of tuberoses, immaculate petals reflecting the glow of the star like a mirror.

It’s a star, ALDEBARAN, one of the brightest in the sky, the second star in the floral constellation imagined by its creator Marc-Antoine Barrois and his long-time accomplice, the perfumer Quentin Bisch. 

On the occasion of the immersive installation MISSION ALDEBARAN, a limited edition of 500 numbered bottles, each signed by Marc-Antoine Barrois, is offered exclusively.



"A fragrance like no other. ”

  • Tuberose absolute
  • Paprika accord
  • Mate
  • Tonka bean

We are committed to reducing our environmental impact while offering quality products. Discover our eco-responsible initiatives:

  • Removal of preservatives and UV filters(including BHT) from October 2020.
  • 100% French manufacturing, guaranteeing local know-how and a low carbon impact.
  • Reduction of plastic packaging: removal of cellophane on our boxes, replaced by a holographic label, saving 1,650 meters of cellophane in one year.
  • Redesigned packaging: replacement of foam cushioning with recyclable cardboard cushioning, reused to limit our ecological footprint.
  • Pre-filling of glass bottles, reducing intermediate steps and waste.

Together, let’s commit to more sustainable consumption that respects our planet.

In an approach of transparency and sincerity, here is the list of ingredients of ALDEBARAN :

ALCOHOL DENAT., PARFUM (FRAGRANCE), AQUA (WATER), METHYL BENZOATE, BENZYL SALICYLATE, COUMARIN, BETA-CARYOPHYLLENE, TERPINOLENE, LIMONENE, PINENE, BENZYL BENZOATE, ALPHA-TERPINENE, BENZYL ALCOHOL, LINALOOL, METHYL SALICYLATE, TERPINEOL

ALDEBARAN has never been reformulated since its launch on April 6, 2025. If we ever had the obligation to reformulate it, we will keep you informed here.

THE IMAGINARY OF ALDEBARAN

Like a luminous star in the night, this new fragrance is a soliflore of milky-faceted tuberose and fresh petals, enhanced by paprika and Tonka beans. ALDEBARAN embodies hope and optimism.

ALDEBARAN takes its name from the brightest star discovered and observed by an 18th-century Arab astronomer. Its name, from the Arabic Al-Debaran, evokes the outstretched hand of one who helps and shows the way. This olfactory experience is a quest for optimism, like going through the looking glass, from shadow to light.

QUENTIN BISCH, THE NOSE OF ALDEBARAN

ALDEBARAN is a luminous fragrance. Quentin Bisch reinterprets the diva of white flowers to restore her freshness and almost absolute purity... The perfumer, still alongside Marc-Antoine Barrois, has pushed the boundaries of this iconic flower, creating an olfactory composition of rare elegance that is both delicate and addictive.

"Born from a bouquet of tuberose, the most fatal of flowers. Work the milky facets and fresh petal notes, sprinkle with a pinch of paprika to redden everything. Tonka bean for addiction."

Quentin Bisch

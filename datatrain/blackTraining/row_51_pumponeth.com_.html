FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about PUMP token, airdrop distribution, and our innovative DeFi ecosystem.

General

What is PUMP token?

PUMP is an innovative memecoin built on Solana blockchain, designed to revolutionize the DeFi space with community-driven governance, sustainable tokenomics, and innovative reward mechanisms. Our mission is to create long-term value for holders while maintaining the fun and engaging nature of meme tokens.

How does the airdrop work?

The PUMP airdrop distributes 500,000 tokens (50% of total supply) to early supporters and active community members. Participants can claim their allocation through our platform by connecting their Solana wallet. Distribution is immediate with no vesting period, ensuring fair and transparent access for all eligible users.

What blockchain is PUMP built on?

PUMP is built on Solana, one of the fastest and most cost-effective blockchains available. This choice ensures lightning-fast transactions with minimal fees, providing an optimal user experience for trading, staking, and participating in our DeFi ecosystem.

Tokenomics

What is the total supply of PUMP?

The total supply of PUMP is 1,000,000 tokens with a deflationary model. Distribution includes 50% for airdrop, 25% for liquidity, 15% for development, and 10% for marketing. No team allocation ensures fair distribution and prevents dumping by insiders.

How does the deflationary model work?

Our deflationary mechanism includes regular token burns and buybacks from trading fees. A percentage of transaction fees is automatically used to purchase tokens from the market and permanently remove them from circulation, creating scarcity and potentially increasing value for remaining holders.

Are there any staking rewards?

Yes! PUMP holders can stake their tokens to earn rewards from multiple sources including trading fees, yield farming returns, and governance incentives. Staking also provides voting power in DAO decisions, giving holders direct influence over the project's future development.

Security

Is PUMP token audited?

Yes, PUMP smart contracts have been thoroughly audited by leading blockchain security firms. Our code undergoes regular security reviews and follows industry best practices to ensure the safety of user funds and the integrity of the protocol.

How secure is the liquidity?

25% of total supply allocated for liquidity is locked for 12 months in audited smart contracts. This ensures stable trading conditions and prevents rug pulls. Liquidity locks are verifiable on-chain and provide long-term security for all traders and holders.

What about team tokens?

PUMP has zero team allocation - no tokens are reserved for founders or team members. This ensures complete fairness and eliminates the risk of insider dumping. The team is committed to the project's success through community growth and long-term value creation.

Trading & Support

Where can I trade PUMP?

PUMP is available on major Solana DEXs including Raydium, Orca, and Jupiter. We're also working on CEX listings to increase accessibility. Always verify contract addresses and use official links to avoid scams and ensure you're trading the legitimate PUMP token.

How do I get support?

Join our active community on Discord and Telegram for real-time support and updates. Our team and community moderators are available 24/7 to help with technical issues, answer questions, and provide guidance on using the PUMP ecosystem.

What's the roadmap for PUMP?

Our comprehensive roadmap includes mobile app development, cross-chain bridge implementation, yield farming launch, NFT marketplace integration, and global expansion. We're currently in Q3 2025 focusing on platform enhancements and preparing for major DeFi features in Q4.
